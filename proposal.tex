\section*{Project Proposal}
Initial idea is to trace sound waves from a sound source placed inside an environment, and to observe how the sound travels through the space and interacts with the environments surfaces.
\subsection*{Desired Features}
    \begin{itemize}
        \item ML Material Classification
        \item Observer Sound Intensity
        \item Optimal Sound Source Location
        \item 3D Scene Renderer
        \item Acoustic Rasterizer
    \end{itemize}
